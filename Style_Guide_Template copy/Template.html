Type

The current typeface I’m using for The Cat Inn, is the varying and multi- purpose weights of Nimbus Sans. A derivative of Helvetica, Nimbus Sans is a Sans-Serif typeface published by German company URW++. I chose Nimbus Sans for it’s effortless and readable type and for its versatility.

Orelo is used for headers and titles to add character to the face of the brand. The geometric shapes compliment that of circular, while the thin serif-like stroke within the typeface provides the ‘cat-like’ appeal. Distinctly, the ‘T’s’ have a character of their own, which adds to the design motif.

The typeface Circular is used for numbers as the curvature and geometric shapes of the symbols make it easy to distinguish, while also having a playful dynamic.

The colour palette’s chosen for this project are meant to reflect the values identified in the moodboard - playful, carefree, happy and safe. Yellow hue’s play a predominant role as they elicit the happy and carefree feelings, however strong yellow is in its appearance, I’ve used monotone colours to compliment the choices.
